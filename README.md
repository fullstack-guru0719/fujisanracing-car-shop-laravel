### Requirements

* **SERVER**: Apache 2 or NGINX.
* **RAM**: 3 GB or higher.
* **PHP**: 7.3 or higher.
* **For MySQL users**: 5.7.23 or higher.
* **For MariaDB users**: 10.2.7 or Higher.
* **Node**: 8.11.3 LTS or higher.
* **Composer**: 1.6.5 or higher.
